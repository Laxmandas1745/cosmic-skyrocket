Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about Cosmic Skyrocket - your trusted partner in tiles and natural stone exports

What does Cosmic Skyrocket do?

Cosmic Skyrocket is a global exporter of tiles and natural stones, supplying premium ceramic, porcelain, sandstone, and limestone products crafted with high production standards.

How long has the company been in the industry?

The company has 28+ years of experience in the building materials and natural stone/tile export segment.

Who are the typical clients or partners?

They work closely with architects, designers, builders, manufacturers, and retailers across the world.

Which countries or regions does Cosmic Skyrocket export to?

The company exports to Schengen, Gulf, Asia-Pacific, Latin America, Middle East, and Mediterranean regions and more than 26 countries globally.

What types of products does the company offer?

Cosmic Skyrocket offers: Tiles (Ceramic, Porcelain), Indian Natural Stones (Sandstone, Limestone), and customized designs, sizes, and finishes based on project requirements.

What is the production capacity?

Tiles: 37,000+ sq. meters/day | Natural Stone Extraction: 230+ cubic meters/day | Manpower: 300+ staff | Dealers in India: 32+ dealers nationwide

Which ports in India do they use for export?

Cosmic Skyrocket is registered at Mundra Port (INMUN) and Nhava Sheva Port (INNSA). These ports are used for handling all export shipments.

Does the company offer customization?

Yes. Clients can request custom tile and natural stone sizes, finishes, and designs, tailored to specific architectural or commercial needs.

What makes Cosmic Skyrocket cost-competitive?

Their India-based manufacturing units provide cost advantages due to: Abundant raw materials, Affordable manpower, and Favorable economic conditions. This allows global brands to import under their own name at competitive rates.

What are the company's core values?

Cosmic Skyrocket focuses on: Quality & innovation, Sustainability, Transparency & integrity, Customer satisfaction, and Long-term partnerships.

What are the accepted payment methods?

The company accepts: Electronic Funds Transfer, Wire / Telex / TT, and Irrevocable Letter of Credit (LC). Optional methods can be agreed upon separately.

Which Incoterms do they support?

Preferred and supported shipping terms include: FOB, CIF, and CNF/CFR. Responsibilities under each Incoterm are clearly defined for buyers and sellers.

What documents are provided for export shipments?

Shipments are supported with all essential documents, such as: Proforma Invoice, Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Certificate of Origin, Bill of Lading, and Insurance Certificate. All documents ensure smooth customs processing and compliance.
